Singapore - Finance · Motorsport · Culture
Singapore Grand Prix - The Mandala Club
The most concentrated week of capital in Asia

A full private buyout of one of Singapore's most exclusive private members' houses, inside the week when Formula One and Token2049 converge. More than one hundred principals by invitation only - founders, allocators, family offices and institutional capital from four continents. The room was designed for one thing. A room made by timing, trust and proximity. Not promotion.
